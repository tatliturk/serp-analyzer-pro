# Privacy Policy — SERP Analyzer Pro

Last updated: March 2026

SERP Analyzer Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- No user data is collected
- No analytics or tracking
- No data is sent to any server
- All processing happens locally in your browser

The `storage` permission is used only to save your display preferences locally on your device.

The `activeTab` permission is used only to inject SEO analysis badges into Google search result pages.

Contact: https://github.com/tatliturk/serp-analyzer-pro/issues
